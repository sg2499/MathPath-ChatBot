# MathPath Admin Operations Guide

## Daily workflow

1. Open the admin dashboard.
2. Refresh the lead inbox.
3. Call hot leads first.
4. Follow up warm leads within the same day.
5. Export leads CSV at the end of the day if required.
6. Review chat logs weekly to find repeated parent questions.

## Lead priority meaning

### Hot lead

The parent is showing high intent. Examples:

- Asked to book a demo
- Asked for admission
- Asked for fees
- Shared phone details
- Asked for callback
- Asked how to join

Recommended action: call immediately or within 1 hour.

### Warm lead

The parent is exploring seriously. Examples:

- Asked about age/class suitability
- Asked about weak maths support
- Asked about Bridge Course
- Asked about daily practice or app tracking

Recommended action: follow up the same day.

### New lead

General enquiry.

Recommended action: send introductory WhatsApp or call during the preferred callback window.

## Suggested call opening

“Hello, this is from MathPath. You had enquired through our website chatbot about your child’s maths learning. May I know your child’s class and what support you are looking for?”

## Suggested WhatsApp follow-up

Hello! Thank you for enquiring about MathPath – Ace with Abacus.  
MathPath combines abacus, visualisation, daily app practice, and school maths support to help children build speed, accuracy, confidence, and stronger maths fundamentals.  
Please let us know a convenient time for a short call so we can guide you to the right level/demo class.

## Weekly improvement checklist

- Review unanswered or repeated questions.
- Add missing answers to the knowledge base.
- Check if parents are asking for fees/batches repeatedly.
- Update FAQ if needed.
- Check if program recommendation is accurate for age/class.
- Export and back up lead CSV.
