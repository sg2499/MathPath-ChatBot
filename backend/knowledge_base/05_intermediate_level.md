# Intermediate Level

## Suitable Learners

The Intermediate Level is suitable for children who have completed foundational or preparatory abacus learning and are ready for advanced application.

It is especially useful for children preparing for stronger middle-school mathematics performance.

## Structure and Duration

The Intermediate Level includes 4 levels, with each level lasting approximately 4 months.

## Purpose

The purpose of the Intermediate Level is to move children from calculation practice toward strategic mathematical application.

This stage strengthens complex operations, multi-step thinking, and problem-solving confidence.

## What Children Learn

The Intermediate Level may include:

- Complex multiplication
- Complex division
- BODMAS
- Decimals
- Integers
- Squares
- Advanced operational structuring
- Faster mental calculations
- Strategic thinking
- Logical reasoning
- School maths application

## How It Helps Children

This program helps children:

- Handle more complex calculations
- Become more accurate under time pressure
- Build exam-readiness
- Improve confidence in middle-school topics
- Use visualisation for complex operations
- Develop flexible mathematical thinking

## Parent-Friendly Explanation

The Intermediate Level is not only about doing bigger sums. It helps children think more strategically, organise steps, reduce errors, and become comfortable with more advanced school mathematics.
