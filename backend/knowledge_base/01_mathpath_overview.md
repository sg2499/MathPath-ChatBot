# MathPath Overview

MathPath, also referred to as Math Path Abacus, is a mathematics learning program for children that combines abacus training, visualisation techniques, school syllabus integration, cognitive development, and regular practice support.

The brand positioning is: **MathPath — Ace with Abacus**.

MathPath is designed to help children build strong mathematical foundations, improve calculation speed and accuracy, develop mental picture memory, and gain confidence in school mathematics. The program uses a hybrid model where children attend structured classes and continue regular practice through daily app-based learning support.

## Core Promise

MathPath helps children unlock their true mathematical potential through a unique hybrid learning experience that blends:

- Abacus-based calculation training
- Visualisation and picture-memory methods
- School syllabus integration
- Daily practice support
- Gamified learning
- Progress tracking
- Timely feedback for parents

The goal is not only to make children faster at calculations but also to make them more confident, disciplined, accurate, and comfortable with mathematical thinking.

## What MathPath Offers

MathPath offers a structured abacus and mathematics learning pathway for children from early childhood to middle-school years. The program supports children from approximately LKG/UKG to Grade 7, depending on the child’s age, level, and existing mathematical foundation.

The curriculum is designed to support school learning across CBSE, ICSE, WBSEB, and State Board patterns.

## Organisational Strength

MathPath is built on more than 17 years of educational experience. The organisation focuses on child-friendly, parent-transparent, and practice-driven mathematics learning.

Unlike conventional abacus programs that may focus only on calculation drills, MathPath connects abacus learning with practical school mathematics, concept clarity, Olympiad readiness, and confidence building.
