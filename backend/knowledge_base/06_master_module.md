# Master Module

## Suitable Learners

The Master Module is designed for advanced learners who are ready to connect abacus and mental maths skills with higher school-level mathematical applications.

## Purpose

The Master Module helps children apply calculation confidence to practical and school-relevant topics.

## What Children Learn

The Master Module may include:

- Percentages
- Square roots
- Cubes
- Cube roots
- Profit and loss
- Decimal multiplication
- Decimal division
- Simple interest
- Advanced calculation applications

## How It Helps Children

The Master Module helps children:

- Apply arithmetic in school-style topics
- Become more confident in higher-level calculation
- Improve speed and accuracy in practical maths
- Strengthen preparation for exams and competitions
- Build confidence in commercially relevant topics like profit, loss, and interest

## Parent-Friendly Explanation

The Master Module connects calculation skills with topics children commonly face in higher classes. It is useful for children who have already built a foundation and now need application-based confidence.
