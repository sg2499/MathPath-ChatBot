# Bridge Course

## What Is the Bridge Course?

The Bridge Course is MathPath’s unique fast-track support for late joiners.

It is especially useful for children who discover abacus or structured mental maths later, such as in Class 5, Class 6, or Class 7, and may not want to start slowly with much younger children.

## Purpose

Many children join abacus programs late. Starting them from the very beginning may feel slow, repetitive, or discouraging. The Bridge Course helps such children cover essential foundational concepts in a structured but faster way.

## Who Should Consider It?

The Bridge Course may be suitable for:

- Children joining late
- Children in Class 5, 6, or 7
- Children who need stronger calculation basics
- Children who are weak in fundamentals but need faster progress
- Children who want to enter the MathPath pathway without unnecessary delay

## How It Helps

The Bridge Course helps by:

- Building missing foundations
- Reducing fear of late joining
- Avoiding unnecessary repetition
- Preparing the child for suitable MathPath levels
- Helping older children catch up confidently
- Supporting better school maths performance
